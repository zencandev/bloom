# Privacy Policy for Bloom

**Last Updated:** [Current Date]

Bloom ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

Bloom is designed to be a private, local-first application.

### Camera and Microphone
To function as a video journal, Bloom requires access to your device's **Camera** and **Microphone**.
*   **Camera:** Used to record video clips for your daily journal.
*   **Microphone:** Used to record ambient audio for your video clips.

**We do not transmit, store, or analyze your video or audio recordings on our servers.** All recordings are stored locally on your device.

### Local Storage
All data, including video clips and app preferences, is stored locally on your device using the device's internal storage. We do not have access to this data.

## 2. Data Sharing

Since we do not collect or store your personal data on our servers, we do not share your personal information with third parties.
