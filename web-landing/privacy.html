Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 13, 2026

Bloom ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Bloom mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

1. Data Collection and Use

Bloom is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect or store your personal data on external servers. All videos, images, and journals you capture are stored locally on your device.

2. Media and Files

Our app requires access to your camera and microphone to record the "daily moments" and access to your photo library to save generated "Weekly Zen" videos. These files are processed locally on your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

Bloom does not utilize third-party tracking or analytics SDKs that collect your personal information. Any data remains within the application container on your mobile device.

4. Data Retention

Your data stays on your device as long as the app is installed. If you uninstall the app, all local data (videos and history) will be deleted unless you have manually saved them to your device's photo library.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Service is intended for a general audience and does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. All data remains on the device.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
